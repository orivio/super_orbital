In the year 20XX, an abnormal child was conceived in a laboratory. A gravitational anomaly, codename LONE_KID, was somehow unaffected by the gravity of the Earth.

Astrophysicists and engineers would spend decades researching Lone Kid's abnormalities. Eventually, renowned scientist Dr. Harfingjingles (iykyk) developed a special suit that allowed Lone Kid to create his own source of gravity, known as the Suit of Artificial Density (S.A.D.).

Years later, Lone Kid, who came to be known as Sad Guy, had a midlife crisis. Artificial gravity wasn't enough for him anymore. After reading a conspiracy on how gravity is actually an artificial force created by a government device which is drilled into the core of every planet, Sad Guy sets out on a mission to take gravity from a distant planet.

Sad Guy uses his S.A.D. to propel into deep space, finding himself on a mars-like planet named Zeta-C5.

Now he needs to travel through the planet's unnaturally hostile environment to fulfill his own personal mission.
